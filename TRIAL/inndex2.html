Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Get Started" button on our homepage, enter your email address, create a strong password, and verify your email. You'll then need to complete identity verification for security purposes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and other cryptocurrencies like Ethereum and Litecoin. Availability may vary by region.

How secure is my money?

Your security is our top priority. We use industry-leading security measures including two-factor authentication, cold storage for 98% of assets, and regular security audits. All digital assets are also insured against theft.

Can I trade on my mobile device?

Yes! We offer dedicated mobile apps for both iOS and Android devices. You can download them from the App Store or Google Play. The mobile app includes all the features of our web platform.

What are your trading fees?

Our standard trading fee is 0.5% per trade. Pro users enjoy reduced fees of 0.2%, while institutional clients have custom volume-based pricing. There are no deposit fees, and withdrawal fees vary by cryptocurrency.
